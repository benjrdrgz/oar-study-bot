Frequently asked questions

The Officer Aptitude Rating (OAR) is the first 3 sections of the ASTB-E (Aviation Selection Test Battery). It measures math skills, reading comprehension, and mechanical comprehension. The OAR is used by the Navy, Marines, and Coast Guard to evaluate candidates for officer programs including OCS, NROTC, and STA-21.

The minimum qualifying score is 35, but competitive scores are 50+. Most successful OCS candidates score between 50 and 60. The higher your score, the stronger your application. OAR Pro is designed to get you above that 50 threshold.

You get 3 lifetime attempts on the ASTB-E (which includes the OAR). The Navy uses your most recent score, not your highest. There is a mandatory wait period between attempts. This is why proper preparation before your first attempt is critical.
