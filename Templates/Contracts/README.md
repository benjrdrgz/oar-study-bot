# Service Agreement Contract v1.0

## Overview
This is the professional client service agreement contract for the AI Local Website Agency. Clients sign this when they purchase website services, covering all terms, conditions, and service tiers.

## File Details
- **Filename**: `Service_Agreement_v1.0.docx`
- **Format**: Microsoft Word (.docx)
- **Font**: Arial throughout
- **Page Size**: US Letter (8.5" × 11")
- **File Size**: ~40 KB

## Contract Structure

### 10 Comprehensive Sections
1. **Parties** — Identifies Agency, Client, and Effective Date
2. **Scope of Services** — Lists all deliverables (website design, hosting, SSL, SEO, etc.)
3. **Service Tiers & Pricing** — Three-tier pricing table with feature comparison
4. **Payment Terms** — Setup fees, monthly billing, late penalties, payment methods
5. **Project Timeline** — Draft delivery (5 days), review period (7 days), go-live (24 hours)
6. **Client Responsibilities** — Information delivery, feedback timelines, domain setup
7. **Intellectual Property** — Agency ownership with $497 transfer fee option
8. **Term & Termination** — 3-month minimum, month-to-month after, 30-day cancellation
9. **Limitation of Liability** — Caps and exclusions for third-party services
10. **Miscellaneous** — Governing law, amendments, severability, contact info

## Customizable Placeholders

Replace these in the contract before sending to clients:

- `[AGENCY_NAME]` — Your agency name (e.g., "Local Web Solutions")
- `[CLIENT_NAME]` — Client's name or primary contact name
- `[CLIENT_BUSINESS_NAME]` — Client's business name
- `[DATE]` — Effective date of agreement (e.g., "March 25, 2026")
- `[STATE]` — Your state for governing law (e.g., "Wisconsin")
- `[BILLING_DAY]` — Day of month for recurring billing (e.g., "15th")
