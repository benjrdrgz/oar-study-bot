# Follow-Up Email Templates After Sales Calls

v1.0 — Post-discovery call templates for hot leads, no-shows, existing clients, and upsells

---

## TEMPLATE 1: AFTER DISCOVERY CALL (Interested, Needs to Think)

**Subject Line Variants:**
- [OWNER_NAME], thanks for taking the time today
- Here's what we discussed for [BUSINESS_NAME]
- Quick recap from our call + next steps

**Body:**

Hi [OWNER_NAME],

Thanks for taking 30 minutes to walk through the strategy today. I really like [SPECIFIC_DETAIL_FROM_CALL — e.g., "how you're thinking about Google visibility in [CITY]"].

Here's what we covered:
- Your current situation: [BRIEF_RECAP]
- The opportunity: [VISIBILITY_ANGLE — e.g., "90 days of ranking advantage before your competitors move"]
- The timeline: Launch within [DAYS] days, visible within [DAYS] days
- Your investment: [TIER] at $[PRICE]

**What happens next:**

If you want to move forward, here's the play:
1. Confirm by [DATE] and I'll lock in your launch slot
2. I'll send over the project agreement + domain setup instructions
3. You've got 48 hours to get me your brand assets (logo, photos, copy preferences)
4. Site launches within 72 hours of that
5. You're live on Google Maps within 2 weeks

No pressure if you need time to think. But the sooner you decide, the sooner your customers find you.

One thing to keep in mind: I'm batching five sites in [CITY] this month. If you're not in that batch, the next opening is [DATE]. So if you're leaning yes, now's the time to lock it in.

Let me know what you're thinking.

— Benjamin Rodriguez

## TEMPLATE 2: AFTER DISCOVERY CALL (Ready to Buy, Sending Invoice)

**Subject Line Variants:**
- [OWNER_NAME], let's get [BUSINESS_NAME] live
- Your project agreement + invoice inside
- Next step: Confirm and we'll start tomorrow

**Body:**

Hi [OWNER_NAME],

Great call today. You're making the right move.

I've put together everything you need to get started:

**Your Project Agreement:** [LINK_TO_AGREEMENT]
- Scope: Full website build + [TIER] strategy
- Timeline: 72-hour initial launch, visible results within 14 days
- Investment: $[PRICE]
- What's included: [BRIEF_LIST]

**Your Invoice:** [LINK_TO_INVOICE]
- Due: [DATE]
- Payment methods: ACH, credit card, or wire transfer
- 50% due to lock in your slot, 50% due at launch

**What I need from you:**

By [DATE], please send:
1. Your business logo (high-res PNG or vector)
2. 5-10 photos (you working, your space, finished work — whatever shows what you do)
3. Your 2-3 key service offerings + 1 sentence description each
4. Your Google Business Profile link (if you have one)
5. Any reviews or testimonials you want featured

**Timeline:**
- [DATE]: Invoice due + brand assets due → I start building
- [DATE]: First draft launch → you review
- [DATE]: Final tweaks + go live
- [DATE]: Site visible on Google Maps + ranking begins

You're locked in. Let's get to work.

Questions? Hit reply.

— Benjamin Rodriguez

## TEMPLATE 3: AFTER NO-SHOW (Rescheduling)

**Subject Line Variants:**
- We missed you, [OWNER_NAME] — let's reschedule
- Your [BUSINESS_NAME] strategy call: rescheduling
- Quick reschedule for our call

**Body:**

Hi [OWNER_NAME],

We were scheduled to jump on a call today at [TIME] to walk through the strategy for [BUSINESS_NAME], but I didn't hear from you.

No drama — things happen. Let's just pick another time.

I'm looking at my calendar and have these windows open:

**Tuesday [DATE]:** 10am, 1pm, 3pm CT
**Wednesday [DATE]:** 9am, 11am, 2pm CT
**Thursday [DATE]:** 10am, 12pm, 4pm CT

Just reply with what works best. We can do Zoom or phone — whatever's easier.

The reason I want to get this on the calendar: you've got a real opportunity right now. Local visibility in [CITY] is wide open. But if we wait too long, your competitors will have already claimed the top spots. So let's lock this in.

Pick a time and we'll make it happen.

— Benjamin Rodriguez

Your website has been live for [TIMEFRAME] now. Traffic's coming in. Phones are ringing. That's great.

Here's what I'm noticing: you're getting traffic from [PRIMARY_KEYWORD], but there's low-hanging fruit in 3-4 other searches where [CITY] customers are actively looking for your services.

Right now, you're getting maybe [X] leads per month. With a focused 90-day push, I think we can get you to [2X].

**Here's the play:**

Upgrade to Tier 2 for $[UPSELL_PRICE]:
- I identify the [X] highest-value keywords in your area
- We create targeted content around each one
- I build local authority signals (citations, reviews, structured data)
- You get a custom strategy doc showing exactly what's driving results
- I handle all the technical setup — you just watch it work

Timeline: 90 days to see [X]% traffic lift.

Most Tier 1 clients see this and upgrade. The ROI is undeniable — you'll be making back the investment in the first month from extra phone calls alone.

Want to lock this in? I've got 2 Tier 2 slots open this month. After that, we wait until next month.

Let me know.

— Benjamin Rodriguez

**Let's jump on a call next week to walk through this deeper.** I'll show you exactly which strategies are driving the most valuable leads.

## QUICK REFERENCE: WHEN TO USE EACH TEMPLATE

| Situation | Template | Key Point |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Call completed, buyer wants to think | Template 1 | Recap + urgency (batching) |
| Call completed, ready to move | Template 2 | Invoice + timeline + what you need |
| Scheduled call was missed | Template 3 | Reschedule immediately + scarcity |
| Month 1+ of live website | Template 4 | Specific traffic/ranking data |
| Client is climbing Tier 1 | Template 5 | Show revenue opportunity |
| Quarterly checkup (Tier 2/3) | Template 6 | Business impact + next quarter plan |
