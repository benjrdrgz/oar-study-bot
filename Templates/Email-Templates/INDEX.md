## How to Use Each Template

### COLD EMAIL TEMPLATE (Primary Sequence)

**When to use:** New prospects with no prior contact

**Workflow:**
1. Create lead CSV (business_name, owner_name, email, city, state, niche, demo_site_url)
2. Run email personalizer script (generates all 5 emails)
3. Send Email #1 on Day 0
4. Auto-send Emails #2-5 on scheduled days

**Subject Line Strategy:**
- Email 1: Use variant A or B (personal touch)
- Email 2: Use variant A (continuity)
- Email 3: Use variant B or C (market angle)
- Email 4: Use variant B (urgency + social proof)
- Email 5: Use variant A or C (final chance)

**Best Practices:**
- Spread Email #1 across 2-3 hours (10-15/hour)
- Send 9am-11am recipient timezone
- No follow-up until Email #3 if no reply
- Track open/click/reply rates per niche

---

### WARM REPLY TEMPLATES (Objection Handlers)

**When to use:** Prospect replies to cold email

**Trigger:**
- Prospect replies with specific question
- Match their question to correct template
- Send personalized response same day

**Reply #1: "How much does it cost?"**
- Shows all 3 tiers + pricing
- Explains what's included in each
- Social proof (most start Tier 1)
- Ends with "Which tier fits?"

**Reply #2: "Tell me more"**
- Shorter 48-hour window
- Walks through technical + business benefits
- Emphasizes ownership + flexibility
- ROI-focused close

**Reply #3: "I'm interested but not right now"**
- Validates timeline concern
- Adds scarcity (batch window)
- Offers to lock in call date
- Positions "next batch" timing

**Reply #4: "Can I see more examples?"**
- Portfolio link
- Walks through 3 key elements (design, SEO, speed)
- Offers screen share demo
- Asks for call

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #1: Thinking Stage

**When to use:** Discovery call completed, prospect is interested but wants time

**Timing:** Send same day after call

**Key Elements:**
1. Specific detail from call (shows you listened)
2. Recap of 4 key points discussed
3. Clear "what happens next" section
4. Batch window scarcity
5. Call to action (confirm by specific date)

**Success Metric:**
- 30-40% respond with "Yes, let's move forward"
- 30% request more time (send again in 3 days)
- 30% no response (send Template #3 or disqualify)

---

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #2: Ready to Buy

**When to use:** Discovery call completed, prospect says "Let's do it"

**Timing:** Send same day (within 1 hour if possible)

**Key Elements:**
1. Project agreement link
2. Invoice (50/50 split: deposit + launch)
3. Clear timeline (Day 1-4)
4. Deliverables checklist (what you need from them)
5. Payment methods
6. No ambiguity — "You're locked in"

**Critical Details:**
- Invoice due date (usually 48 hours)
- Brand assets needed (logo, photos, descriptions)
- When you start building
- When site goes live
- When visibility begins

**Success Metric:**
- 80%+ return signed agreement within 48 hours
- 70%+ return brand assets on time
- 90%+ complete payment within 5 days

---

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #3: No-Show

**When to use:** Scheduled call was missed, prospect didn't reach out

**Timing:** Send same day within 1 hour of missed call

**Tone:** Professional + non-judgmental

**Key Elements:**
1. Acknowledge miss without blame
2. 3 new time options (next 3 days)
3. Casual "whatever works" framing
4. Restate why this matters (scarcity)
5. Ask for confirmation

**Success Metric:**
- 40-60% reschedule within 24 hours
- 20-30% reschedule within 3-5 days
- 10-20% never reschedule (disqualify)

**If they reschedule:** Send reminder 24 hours before new call

---

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #4: Monthly Check-In

**When to use:** Website has been live 30+ days with active client

**Timing:** Send on Day 30, 60, 90 (same day each month)

**Key Elements:**
1. Traffic % change (vs. last month)
2. Top ranking keyword + position
3. Google Maps visibility (weekly searches)
4. Calls from website (number)
5. Mobile traffic %
6. "What's working" (2-3 items)
7. "What we should adjust" (1-2 items)
8. Next 30-day improvements
9. CTA: "Let's hop on a call to dig deeper"

**Success Metric:**
- 30-50% open this email (high engagement)
- 10-20% respond requesting deeper analysis
- Sets up upsell conversation (Tier 1 → Tier 2)

**Pro Tip:** Include screenshots of Google Analytics or Search Console if possible

---

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #5: Upsell (Tier 1 → Tier 2)

**When to use:** Tier 1 client seeing traction, ready to scale

**Trigger:**
- 30-60 days after launch
- Website is generating leads/calls
- Owner is happy with momentum
- Ready to invest more

**Key Elements:**
1. Acknowledge momentum ("Traffic's coming in, phones are ringing")
2. Identify untapped opportunity (3-4 other high-value keywords)
3. Show math (current leads vs. potential)
4. Tier 2 pitch (90-day focused push)
5. Timeline (90 days to see lift)
6. Scarcity (2 slots open this month)
7. ROI-focused close

**Success Metric:**
- 40-60% of Tier 1 clients upgrade within 90 days
- Average deal size: $2,500 + $3,000 = $5,500 ACV

---

### POST-CALL TEMPLATE #6: Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

**When to use:** Tier 2/3 client at 90-day mark and beyond

**Timing:** Send quarterly (Day 90, 180, 270, 360)

**Key Elements:**
1. Executive summary (calls generated, revenue impact)
2. By-the-numbers section (traffic, rankings, visibility)
3. "Here's what drove those results" (4 key strategies)
4. Next quarter plan (what's changing)
5. New opportunities (A/B tests, new strategies)
6. Client action items (reviews, testimonials)
7. "Let's jump on a call to dig deeper"

**Success Metric:**
- 70%+ open rate (established relationship)
- 40-50% schedule QBR call
- 80%+ renew for next quarter
- Sets up renewal conversation

## Customization Tips

### Making Templates Your Own

1. **Adjust tone:** More formal or casual?
2. **Adjust length:** Shorter or longer?
3. **Adjust urgency:** Increase or decrease?
4. **Adjust CTA:** What do you want them to do?
5. **Adjust specific details:** Niche language, local references

### Testing

1. Create 2-3 subject line variants per email
2. Test with 100+ emails minimum
3. Run for 2 weeks before declaring winner
4. Rotate winners into full campaigns
