Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

▼

We have three packages: Quick Start at $697, Growth at $1,497 (most popular), and Dominate at $2,497. That's a one-time fee — no monthly charges, no contracts, and you own the website forever. One customer inquiry typically pays for the entire site.

How long does it take?

▼

We build a preview of your website in 24 hours. You review it, request any changes, and then we launch it. Most clients go live within 48 hours total. No waiting 6 months like traditional agencies.

Do I need to provide anything?

▼

Just your business info and photos. If you don't have photos, we can use placeholders and you can update them later. You don't need to know anything about web design or tech — that's our job.

What if I don't like it?

▼

We'll revise the design until you love it. But honestly, most clients are amazed by the preview and only ask for small tweaks. We don't release your site until you say it's perfect.

Can I cancel anytime?

▼

There's nothing to cancel. You own the website outright. We can help you maintain it if you want, but there's no lock-in, no monthly billing, and no surprise fees. It's yours forever.

Do you help with Google?

▼

Yes. Every website comes optimized for Google search. We set up your Google Business Profile, optimize your pages, and submit your site to Google. You'll start showing up in local search results.
