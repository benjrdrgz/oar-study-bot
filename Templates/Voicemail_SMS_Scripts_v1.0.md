## VOICEMAIL SCRIPTS
Read these word-for-word. Keep under 30 seconds each.

### VM1: First Contact (after email sent)
"Hi [NAME], this is Benjamin with [AGENCY_NAME]. I sent you an email earlier — I actually went ahead and built a website for [BUSINESS NAME]. It's already live at [URL]. Take a look when you get a chance and let me know what you think. My number is [PHONE]. Again, that's Benjamin, [PHONE]. Talk soon."

### VM2: Follow-up (no response to email or VM1)
"Hey [NAME], Benjamin again. Just checking if you got a chance to see the website I built for [BUSINESS NAME]. It came out really sharp. The link is in the email I sent, or just text me and I'll send it again. [PHONE]. Have a great day."

### VM3: After They Viewed the Demo (if tracking shows click)
"Hi [NAME], I noticed you checked out the preview site for [BUSINESS NAME] — glad you took a look! If you want to make any changes or get it live with your own domain, I can have that done in 24 hours. Give me a call at [PHONE] or just text me. Talk soon."

### VM4: Hot Lead (they replied positively but haven't committed)
"[NAME], Benjamin here. Got your message — I'm excited to get [BUSINESS NAME] live. I've got a few quick questions about how you'd like things set up. Can you call me back at [PHONE]? I'm free [TIME RANGE] today. Looking forward to it."

### VM5: Breakup (final attempt)
"Hey [NAME], last call from me — I'm clearing out preview sites this week and wanted to check if you want to keep the one I built for [BUSINESS NAME] before I remove it. If so, just text me at [PHONE]. If not, no worries at all. I wish you the best with the business."

## RULES FOR VOICEMAIL & SMS
- Always be warm and casual, never salesy
- Keep voicemails under 30 seconds
- Keep SMS under 160 characters when possible (for non-smartphone delivery)
- Always include your phone number in voicemails (say it twice)
- Never send more than 2 SMS without a response
- Best times: Tues-Thurs, 10am-11am and 2pm-4pm local time
- If they say stop/unsubscribe in SMS, stop immediately (CAN-SPAM/TCPA)
