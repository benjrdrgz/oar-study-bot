v1.0 — 10 video scripts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

These are screen-recording scripts where you record your screen while building a website, with voiceover. Each video is 30-60 seconds.

---

## VIDEO 1: "I built a website for a random plumber in 15 minutes"

**Hook (first 3 sec):** "I just built this plumber a website for free. Here's what happened."

**Script:** Show the process — Google Maps → find business → open Lovable/template → customize → deploy. Speed up the middle. End with: "Now I'm going to email him the link. If he wants it, we go live in 24 hours."

**CTA:** "Follow for more — I'm building 100 local businesses free websites this month."

---

## VIDEO 2: "This restaurant has 4.8 stars and NO WEBSITE"

**Hook:** "This restaurant has 200 five-star reviews and you can't find them on Google."

**Script:** Show the Google Maps listing. Show the missing website. Show the reviews. "So I built them one." Show the demo site. "This took me 20 minutes."

**CTA:** "If your business doesn't have a website, comment your business name. I might build yours next."

---

## VIDEO 3: "How much money are you losing without a website?"

**Hook:** "If you own a local business without a website, you're losing $[X] every month."

**Script:** Walk through the ROI calculator math. "[Business type] in [city] gets [X] searches per month. That's [Y] potential customers. At $[Z] per job, that's $[total] you're invisible to."

**CTA:** "Drop your business type below and I'll calculate yours."

---

## VIDEO 4: "I cold emailed 50 businesses and THIS happened"

**Hook:** "I emailed 50 businesses their own free website. Here's what happened."

**Script:** Share results — open rate, reply rate, quotes from replies. Show the positive ones AND the rejections (keep it real).

**CTA:** "Follow the journey — I'm building an agency from $0."

---

## VIDEO 5: "Why I build the website BEFORE asking"

**Hook:** "Other agencies send proposals. I send the finished product."

**Script:** Explain the strategy. "When you show someone their own website with their name on it, they can't unsee it." Show an example.

**CTA:** "Business owners — if I built you a free website, would you want to see it?"

---

## VIDEO 6: "Wix vs. me — which looks better?"

**Hook:** "Business owner paid $20/mo for Wix. I built this in 15 minutes."

**Script:** Side-by-side comparison of a Wix template site vs your custom site. Highlight mobile, speed, design.

**CTA:** "If you're paying for Wix, let me show you what you could have instead."

---

## VIDEO 7: "Day in the life of an AI web design agency owner"

**Hook:** "Running a web design agency with AI — here's what my day looks like."

**Script:** Morning routine → scrape leads → build 3 sites → send outreach → handle replies → close a deal. Keep it aspirational but real.

**CTA:** "This business model works because AI does the heavy lifting. Follow for the playbook."

---

## VIDEO 8: "I made $X in my first month with zero experience"

**Hook:** "I started a web design agency with $0 and no coding experience."

**Script:** Share the real numbers. Transparent about costs, revenue, time invested. Show the tools.

**CTA:** "Full breakdown in my next video. Follow so you don't miss it."

---

## VIDEO 9: "The email that gets 40% of my closes"

**Hook:** "This one email closes more deals than all my other emails combined."

**Script:** Reveal the breakup email strategy. "I tell them I'm removing their site. Loss aversion kicks in." Show a real reply (redacted).

**CTA:** "Save this — it works for any service business."

---

## VIDEO 10: "5 businesses that NEED a website in your city"

**Hook:** "Open Google Maps right now. I guarantee you'll find 5 businesses with no website in 2 minutes."

**Script:** Do it live. Search a niche. Point out the businesses missing website buttons. "Each of these is a $1,000-$3,000 client."

**CTA:** "Try this yourself and comment how many you find."

---

## VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

- Record with screen capture (Loom free, OBS free, or QuickTime)
- Add captions (CapCut free)
- Vertical format (9:16) for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Post consistently: 3-5x per week
- Best posting times: 7-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm
- Use trending sounds on TikTok/Reels
- Cross-post everything across all platforms
