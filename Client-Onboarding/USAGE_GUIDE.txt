TONE & APPROACH

This packet is designed to:
✓ Make clients feel excited and valued
✓ Set clear expectations (6-day timeline)
✓ Remove anxiety (no hidden fees, you own it)
✓ Encourage immediate action (email checklist)
✓ Build trust (dedicated contact, transparent process)

---

TIPS FOR MAXIMUM IMPACT

1. Send within 24 hours of contract signing
2. Pair with a welcome email explaining timeline
3. Include link to onboarding portal (if available)
4. Print copies for in-person meetings
5. Follow up with first kick-off call within 2 days
