## Important Notes for Benjamin

### Legal Review
This contract is a comprehensive business template, but I recommend having a local attorney review it before your first client use. They can:
- Verify it complies with your state's laws
- Add state-specific language if needed
- Adjust liability caps if desired
- Review IP ownership terms

### Key Contract Decisions Made

**1. IP Ownership (Section 7)**
- Agency keeps design/code during active subscription
- Client can purchase full ownership for $497
- This protects your competitive advantage and prevents races-to-bottom pricing

**2. Payment Protection (Section 4)**
- Setup fees non-refundable (protects against churn)
- 1.5% monthly late fee (incentivizes on-time payment)
- Service suspension after 30 days overdue (enforcement mechanism)

**3. Service Suspension (Section 4.5)**
- You can suspend hosting/maintenance if payment is 30+ days overdue
- This is a powerful enforcement tool — clients will pay to stay online
- Consider sending a warning email at day 15 before suspending

**4. Revision Limits (Section 3)**
- Quick Start: 1 round (prevents endless changes)
- Growth: 3 rounds (reasonable for $1,997 setup)
- Dominate: Unlimited (justifies premium pricing)

**5. Ownership Transfer (Section 7.3)**
- $497 one-time fee to transfer full ownership
- This creates a second revenue stream without additional work
- Some clients will pay to "own" their site after initial term

### Governing Law (Section 10.1)
The placeholder is `[STATE]`. Replace with your state:
- If you operate from Wisconsin, use "Wisconsin"
- If you have clients in multiple states, consider using "Wisconsin" for simplicity
- Consult your attorney on this decision
