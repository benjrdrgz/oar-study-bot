# Standard Operating Procedures — AI Local Website Agency
> v2.0 — Populated from Master Operating Blueprint

## Business Overview
- **Business Name:** AI Local Website Agency
- **Primary Revenue Model:** Website build fees ($500-$2,000+) + monthly maintenance subscriptions ($100-$300/mo) + email hosting ($20-$60/mo) + visibility optimization
- **Core Product/Service:** AI-assisted discovery of local businesses with weak online presence, demo website generation, personalized outreach, and recurring web services

## Two Operational Pipelines

### Pipeline 1: Lead Acquisition Machine
1. **Discover** — Scan Google Places API + Yelp API + local directories by ZIP code
2. **Score** — Rate each business 0-100 (no website, broken site, outdated design, poor mobile, weak CTA, missing service pages, strong niche economics, contactability)
3. **Tier** — A (80-100), B (60-79), C (40-59). Focus outreach on Tier A and B only.
4. **Generate** — Create demo preview website from niche template: businessname-preview.domain.com
5. **Outreach** — Claude generates personalized email: "I noticed your business doesn't have a modern website. I created a preview for you here: [link]."
6. **Classify** — Auto-classify replies (high interest → "Ready To Call" stage)

### Pipeline 2: Client Operations
1. Onboard — Connect domain, activate hosting, enable SSL
2. Customize — Adjust template to client specs (homepage, services, about, contact, map, lead forms)
3. Launch — Go live within 24 hours of close
4. Monitor — Automated uptime, SSL, broken page, load speed checks
5. Bill — Monthly recurring via invoice system

## Sales Process
1. **Lead Source:** Google Places API, Yelp API, local directories, chamber of commerce listings
2. **Qualification Criteria:** Lead score 60+ (Tier A or B), contactable, niche with strong economics
3. **Proposal/Quote Process:** Demo preview site already built before first contact — show don't tell
4. **Close Process:** Benjamin performs final sales action — phone call or visit, show preview, offer customization, close agreement
5. **Onboarding New Client:** Connect domain, activate hosting, generate product brief (niche, services, USPs, competitors), customize and launch site

## Niche Templates (10 Families)
Restaurants, Barbers, Contractors, Dentists, Gyms, Auto Repair, Med Spas, Roofers, HVAC, Cleaning Services

## Geographic Coverage Strategy
- Track by ZIP code: Untouched → In Progress → Covered → Hot Market → Low Yield → Do Not Revisit
- Dashboard shows: businesses scanned, leads qualified, outreach sent, replies received, clients closed
- Expand systematically — saturate one city before moving to the next

## Client Health Monitoring
- Site uptime, SSL validity, broken pages, page load speed — automated alerts on failure
- Visibility score (0-100): website quality + listings coverage + review volume + SEO presence + content depth
- Improving visibility score demonstrates ongoing service value to clients

## Escalation Path
- **Level 1 (AI handles):** Lead scoring, outreach generation, template website builds, email classification
- **Level 2 (Benjamin reviews):** High-interest lead follow-up, client customization requests, billing issues
- **Level 3 (Benjamin decides):** Pricing exceptions, refund requests, geographic expansion decisions

## Emergency Procedures
- **If client site goes down:** Auto-alert triggers, diagnose hosting/DNS/SSL, restore within 4 hours
- **If client wants to leave:** Offer ownership transfer ($300-$500), provide full export and documentation
- **If lead pipeline dries up:** Expand to new ZIP codes, add new niche templates, increase outreach volume
