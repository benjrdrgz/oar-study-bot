## What This Business Is
AI-powered local website agency. We build professional websites for local businesses (plumbers, salons, restaurants, dentists, auto shops) who have no website or a bad one. We build the site BEFORE contacting them and email them the live preview link. The ones who respond are pre-sold.

## The Three Machines
1. **Lead Factory** — Find businesses without websites. Tool: Outscraper → Google Sheets.
2. **Site Factory** — Build demo sites in 15-20 min. Tools: Lovable.dev, HTML templates, Netlify hosting.
3. **Sales Factory** — Cold email with demo links. Tool: Instantly.ai, 5-email sequence over 14 days.
